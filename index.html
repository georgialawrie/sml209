Bonjour et bienvenue!

This is a fun website created for children aged 10 to 14 with a beginner level of French. You will find lots of interesting and fun facts about France. This website will help you learn useful phrases to know if you are going on holiday with your family/friends to France. There are also exercises themed around going to the beach and going out to a French restaurant so you can impress your family/friends with your new knowledge of the language when on holiday!

Visiting France!

France is a great place to visit, there are lots of lovely beaches and beautiful cities to explore. Paris, the capital, has lots of great museums to visit and you can see the views from the top of the Effiel Tower. Below is a video all about the Effiel Tower!
