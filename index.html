Bonjour et bienvenue!

This is a fun website created for children aged 10 to 14 with a beginner level of French. You will find lots of interesting and fun facts about France. This website will help you learn useful phrases to know if you are going on holiday with your family/friends to France. There are also exercises themed around going to the beach and going out to a French restaurant so you can impress your family/friends with your new knowledge when on holiday!
