About France

This is a fun website for kids so you can learn some fun facts about France, French culture and start to learn French phrases to impress people with on your holidays in France with your family and friends!

Visiting France

France is a great place to visit on holiday, there are lots of lovely beaches and beautiful cities to explore. Paris, the capital, has lots of great museums to visit and you can see the views from the top of the Effiel Tower. But don't forgot about the other cities: Rennes, Bordeaux, Lyon, Toulouse, Marseille....
