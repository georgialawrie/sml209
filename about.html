About France

France is quite a big country; it is 643,801 km² and over twice the size of England! It's divided into 13 regions and each region has it's own personality, it's own foods, produce, dialect and traditions! You can see the names of the regions and how they are divided up on this map of France:

Fun French facts!

Did you know there are more than 66.7 million people that live in France? 2.2 million of those people live in Paris.

The currency is the euro and their flag is known as the 'tricolore' which means three colours as there are three colours in the flag; red, white and blue.

French isn't just spoken in France! It is also spoken in the francophone countries; a few are of these are: Belgium, Canda, Madagascar, Réunion, Switzerland, Seychelles, and Algeria
